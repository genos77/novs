About the firm

We act for clients whose legal interests sit across more than one jurisdiction — and whose matters demand both the standards of UK practice and the depth of local knowledge abroad.

Crossbridge has built a quiet reputation among private clients, family offices and small businesses for handling complex inheritance, property, family and commercial matters in Pakistan, India, Nepal, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka and Türkiye — without requiring our clients to leave the United Kingdom.

We instruct senior counsel in each jurisdiction, supervise the work to UK standards, and report back to our clients in plain English. One point of contact. One fee structure. One file.
